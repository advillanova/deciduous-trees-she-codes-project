Deciduous Trees

and their magic color changing

Are you also enchanted by the magic of color changing trees in the fall? These magical color changing trees are called deciduous, which means: "falling off at maturity", just like our baby teeth, for instance. The color changing process, on the other hand, is caused by the ability of trees, as well as other plants and beings, to perceive the weather and sunlight adjustment, and so, changing their leaf color and, finally, falling.
